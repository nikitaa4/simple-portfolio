Everyday Coach Chatbot App

Worked within the Adaptive Cognition and Interaction Design (ACTION) Lab at UIUC under Dr. Jessie Chin, building significant parts of an app using Flutter and Firebase for research on whether chatbots can be used to effectively support patients with spinal cord injuries. I was the team project manager as well as a developer, and I implemented user authentication, made UI changes, and resolved complex dependency conflicts.

Technologies used include:
